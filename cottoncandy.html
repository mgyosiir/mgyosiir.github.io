History of Cotton Candy

Cotton candy, also known as candy floss, is a classic treat with a history dating back to the 19th century. Its light and fluffy texture is achieved through a process of melting and spinning sugar, creating thin strands that resemble cotton.

Making Cotton Candy at Home

With modern cotton candy machines, it's easy to create your own fluffy clouds of sweetness at home. Experiment with different flavors and colors to make unique cotton candy creations for parties and gatherings.
